Isle of Dogs

A Wes Anderson Film

Official Trailer

Isle of Dogs is a stop-motion animated film directed by Wes Anderson. Set in a dystopian Japan, it tells the story of a boy searching for his dog on Trash Island. The film blends quirky storytelling with stunning visuals and a heartfelt journey of loyalty and courage.
